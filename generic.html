About Spikey Assistance

Speech recognition (Spikey), also known as automatic speech recognition (ASR) or speech-to-text conversion, is a technology that converts spoken language into written text. It is a subfield of natural language processing (NLP) and involves the analysis and processing of audio signals to transcribe spoken words
